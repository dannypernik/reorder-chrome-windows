Privacy Policy for Reorder Chrome Windows

Last updated: November 15, 2025

Reorder Chrome Windows (“the Extension”) is a browser extension designed to let users view, rename, and reorder open Chrome windows. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Extension collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

⸻

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The Extension does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Specifically, we do not collect:
• Browsing history
• Personal information
• Identifiers
• IP addresses
• Cookies
• Website content
• Page titles
• Tab URLs
• Analytics or usage statistics
• Any data that can identify you

The Extension does not communicate with external servers.

⸻

2. Information Processed Locally

To function, the Extension interacts with your browser’s open windows.
All processing happens only on your device, entirely within your browser.

The Extension may:
• Read the list of open Chrome windows
• Read the tabs in each window
• Store the order of windows (locally)
• Store custom window names or settings (locally)

All stored data uses Chrome’s storage.local API and stays on your device. If Chrome Sync is enabled, Chrome may sync this data across your own devices. The developer cannot access synced data.

⸻

3. How Data Is Used

The Extension only uses your data to provide core functionality:
• Display your open windows
• Allow you to reorder windows
• Let you rename windows
• Restore your saved window order
• Save your local preferences

The Extension does not use this data for any other purpose.

⸻

4. No Data Sharing

The Extension does not collect or transmit data, so it does not share any information with third parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or anyone else.

⸻

5. Permissions Explanation

tabs

Required to read information about your open windows and tabs so they can be displayed and reordered.

storage

Used to save local preferences such as custom names and window order.

commands (optional)

Allows assigning a keyboard shortcut to activate the Extension.

The Extension never reads website content or personal browsing data beyond what Chrome provides for window/tab structure.

⸻

6. Children’s Privacy

This Extension does not target or collect information from children and is safe for users of all ages.

⸻

7. Changes to This Policy

If this Policy is updated, the “Last updated” date at the top will change. All changes will maintain the Extension’s commitment to not collect or share personal data.
